Deposits

Fixed Deposits and Recurring Deposits are the easiest tool to get a 6-7% return on your Money.

FDs( Often known as "Mummy - Papa ka Pyar") is the most invested asset. With over 95% Indian Families Invested.

Precious Metals.

Ever Saw a marriage without the passing of inheritanced gold to the young couple? Didn't think so.

Metals like Gold and Silver can easily give a return about 9-10% annually. With more than 65% Indian Families Invested.

Bonds & Schemes

Did you know that you can now invest in various governments bonds and schemes?

From National Pension Schemes(NPS) to Unit linked Insurance Plans(ULIP), we have got your back.

Stocks and IPOs

Stocks and IPOs are one of the best investment Strategies one can plan for long term to be wealthy. I assure you that you won't see a wealthy person without invested in stock market.

Around 3% of the Indians invested in the market, one can find healthy ones among them.

Real Estate

The first thing that anyone would do as investment would be buying his own house.I mean where would you live if you dont have roof above your head? Buying your own house, gives us a Satisfaction of being Successfull.

Around 99% Indians invested in Real Estate.

Mutual Funds

New to equity market? Or dont know where to invest in the market? Big AMCs got you covered.

Taking your money and investing, these companies pays you the profit after a small commission. They buy and sell stocks in huge amount,making a strong difference in supply or demand of stock.

Cryptos & Nfts

With the lastest technology-Web3 & Blockchain, listening about the trending news of cryptos and nfts is not something new. With High return, comes high risk.

With around only 15% of Indians invested, this sure is a mode of investment to think of.!

Startups & Small Cap stocks

Building your own Business and getting a return on the invested capital is sure a dream of almost entire humanity.

Sure, Business & Startups can return multifold return, but thinking about its risk is not something to fail at. It sure teaches a lifelong memorable lessons.!

Forex & Options Trading

Trading is something with which you may become a crorepatti, in just one night. Though Many people trades as full-time worker, and some as Part-Time, the rewards you receive are just...

With many people considering this as a gambling only about 3% Indians use this mode.
